DIABETES

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects how your body processes blood sugar. It can lead to serious health complications if not managed properly. Our platform uses CART algorithm to analyze factors like age, blood pressure, BMI, and more to predict your likelihood of developing diabetes. By providing you with this information, we aim to empower you to take proactive steps towards preventing or managing diabetes. Take charge of your health today.
